B2B Solution Architecture
How SkenPay Fits Into
Your Business.

SkenPay operates as a middleware infrastructure layer — connecting your wallet platform to the world's QR networks through REST API or H5 integration, at both the app and backend level.

REST API Integration

Your backend calls /v1/qr/generate → SkenPay returns a universal QR string → your app renders it → SkenPay routes the scan to the correct network automatically.

H5 Web Integration

No SDK needed. Redirect or embed pay.skenpay.io/checkout?ref=xxx in your app or browser. SkenPay handles the QR display, wallet selection, and payment confirmation.

Webhook Callbacks

After payment, SkenPay POSTs a signed event to your /notify endpoint with transaction status, FX rate, and settlement reference — so your system updates in real time.

About SkenPay
The world runs on QR payments.
We built the infrastructure
that connects them all.

SkenPay was founded by payment professionals who saw the same problem play out across every continent: QR payments were thriving locally, but moving money across borders by QR was still broken. Each network was its own island. Every new market meant a new integration, a new compliance stack, a new settlement arrangement.

We built SkenPay to solve this — not for consumers, but for the companies that build for consumers. Our B2B infrastructure platform gives any wallet, fintech, or payment company a single, production-grade connection to the world's QR ecosystem. One API. One partner. Every QR, everywhere.
